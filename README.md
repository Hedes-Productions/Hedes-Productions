### 👨‍🔬 About Me

Hi, I'm **Tharindu** — a Graduate Research Assistant at the **Intelligent Service Robotics Group**, University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

My research sits at the intersection of **dynamics modelling**, **reinforcement learning**, and **real-world robot deployment**. I'm currently pursuing my **M.Sc. (Major Component of Research)** on electric wheelchairs with non-configurable caster wheels — covering prototype design, caster dynamics characterisation, and **RL with residual networks for trajectory correction via sim-to-real transfer**.

- 🎓 **M.Sc. (Research)** · University of Moratuwa *(2024 – present)*
- ⚡ **B.Sc. (Hons.) Electrical Engineering** · GPA 3.56 · Dean's List · 2nd Sem SGPA 3.97
- 🏆 **Best Poster Award** · ICIPRoB 2026
- 📄 **3 peer-reviewed publications** · 1 IEEE (MERCon 2024) · 2 ICIPRoB 2026
- 🤝 Co-founded **Crytec Labs** · Interned at **GoTradie**, Sydney 🇦🇺
- 🌍 Moratuwa, Sri Lanka · 🇱🇰 Sinhala · 🇬🇧 English · 🇫🇮 Finnish (A2)
- 📬 **Actively seeking PhD opportunities in Robotics**

## 🚀 Featured Project — ARoBotX

> **AR Indoor Navigation & Robot Control** · Final Year Research Project · Jun 2023 – Jun 2024

Built an immersive interface for remote robot operators using **Augmented Reality**, computer vision, and **AWS IoT**. Features real-time 3D environment mapping, 3D hand gesture-based robot control, and global low-latency operation.
