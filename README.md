## 🔬 About Me

I'm a **Graduate Research Assistant** at the [Intelligent Service Robotics Group](https://uom.lk/elect/group/robotics/people), University of Moratuwa, pursuing my **M.Sc. (Major Component of Research)** in robotics. My work focuses on building intelligent robotic systems that operate reliably under real-world constraints.

**Research Areas:**
`Mobile Robotics` `Reinforcement Learning` `Sim-to-Real Transfer` `Motion Control` `Computer Vision` `Augmented Reality` `Reconfigurable Robots`
